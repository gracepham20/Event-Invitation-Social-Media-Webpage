Welcome to irLfg!

irLfg stands for in real life looking for group.

The purpose of this platform is to get people connecting and interacting with each other in their communities in various activities.

For example say you want to play soccer at the park but dont know who to invite or if anyone else near by would want to play as well, you could create an event to ask either just your friends or make a public posting meaning anyone could join
